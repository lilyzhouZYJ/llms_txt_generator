# Profound

> Profound is a comprehensive marketing platform designed to enhance brand visibility in AI-driven search environments. It offers tools like Answer Engine Insights and Agent Analytics to help businesses understand and optimize their presence across various AI platforms. With features tailored for enterprises, content teams, and PR professionals, Profound empowers users to track AI interactions, analyze consumer behavior, and improve their marketing strategies. The platform is built for the future of marketing, ensuring brands remain competitive in an increasingly AI-centric landscape.

## Features
- [Answer Engine Insights: #1 AI Search Visibility Platform](https://www.tryprofound.com/features/answer-engine-insights): Answer Engine Insights provides tools to track and analyze how AI mentions a brand, helping businesses improve their visibility and understand AI-generated narratives. It offers insights into citation sources and competitive benchmarking.
- [AI Crawler & Traffic Analysis | Agent Analytics](https://www.tryprofound.com/features/agent-analytics): Agent Analytics allows businesses to track AI traffic to their sites, providing insights into how AI systems interact with content and the resulting human traffic. It includes features for monitoring AI crawler activity and optimizing site performance.
- [Prompt Volumes - Profound](https://www.tryprofound.com/features/prompt-volumes): Prompt Volumes offers insights into consumer interactions with AI, helping brands improve their visibility by analyzing billions of data signals. It enables businesses to track relevant keywords and topics across AI platforms.
- [Shopping](https://www.tryprofound.com/features/shopping): The Shopping feature enhances product visibility in AI-powered platforms like ChatGPT, allowing users to monitor sentiment, analyze products, and track keyword performance to improve traffic and conversions.
- [Agents](https://www.tryprofound.com/features/agents): Profound's Agents enable marketing teams to automate workflows and extend brand reach through customizable templates and AI-optimized content generation, enhancing overall marketing efficiency.
